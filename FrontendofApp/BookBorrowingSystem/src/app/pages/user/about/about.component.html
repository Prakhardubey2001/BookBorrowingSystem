About Book Borrowing App

Welcome to the Book Borrowing App, your go-to platform for borrowing and lending books with ease.

Features

  • Browse and borrow books from a diverse collection.
  • Conveniently add new books to the system.
  • Manage your borrowed books and lending transactions.

How It Works

  1. In home you can only see the books but can't perform any action on this
  2. You can regiter as a user
  3. You can login as a user
  4. Search for books based on various criteria such as author genre and name and it will show only those books which are avilable (not issued bysomeone).
  5. Borrow books by using your available tokens. If you have no tokens then you can't borrow a book
  6. You can create your own book in Add Book menu in dashboard and you can't borrow your own book
  7. You initially have 4 tokens and you will get one token each time somone borrow your books
  8. you can edit or delete only those books which are created by you and is available (not borrowed bysomeone)
  9. You can see all the details of the book in show books
  10. View and manage your borrowed books in the borrow book menu in dashboard.
